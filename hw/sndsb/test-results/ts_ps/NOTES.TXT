(preliminary notes)

Sound Blaster 16 AWE 64:
 - Supports 4-bit ADPCM, up to 20KHz. Above 20KHz, the card plays at 20KHz with
   jitter/artifact noise in the audio for some reason.
 - Supports 2.6-bit ADPCM, up to 19KHz. Above 19KHz, the card plays at 19KHz with
   jitter/artifact noise in the audio for some reason.
 - Supports 2-bit ADPCM, up to 24KHz. Above 24KHz, the card plays at 24KHz with
   jitter/artifact noise in the audio for some reason.
